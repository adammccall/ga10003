How to use the right click contect menu



  1. Stop video - This stops the persons video

    2. This stops the persons video

  2. Chat

    3. Chat to the person

  3. Rename

    4. renames the person

  4. Pin

    5. pins the persons video box

  5. Make Host

    6. make the person the host

  6. Make Co-Host

    7. makes the person the co host

  7. Allow to Multi-Pin

    8. allow the person to multi pin

  8. Assign to type Closed Caption
  9. Remove
  10. Report
  11. Put in Waiting Room

    12. place back in the waiting room - don't worry you can admit them back in again later.
